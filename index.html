About IntegrateIT Australia

IntegrateIT Australia is a leading provider of IT solutions and support to business’ within Australia. We look at increasing efficiency, productivity and profitability for all of our clients through increased uptime and hardware/software quality. We work with you to get the most out of your IT infrastructure. Our proactive approach allows us to eliminate most technical issues within your network before they occur.

Support

When you are on a support plan with IntegrateIT, you are assigned an engineer as your primary contact. We come out on a scheduled monthly visit and check your network and infrastructure for any issues.

Disaster Recovery

A disaster recovery (DR) plan in essence is a contingency plan for when unforeseen catastrophes are cast upon your business. It is not just data loss you need to be concerned with, but also hardware loss and replacement costs, and the downtime your business and staff will experience.

Managed Services

When one of the servers in a network goes down in your environment, everything grinds to a halt. Many IT service providers offer the traditional “Break Fix” service, waiting around until something goes wrong. IntegrateIT Australia would rather your company not suffer a loss of productivity, that's why our service is highly proactive and we focus on preventing problems.

Auditing/Consulting

We provide audits of your infrastructure to detect issues and possible future issues. We will provide a full written report of the audit for you to keep.

Our team of professionals are ready to take your call. Our commitment to you is to keep improving your business and taking your business to the next level.
