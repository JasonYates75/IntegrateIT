About Us

At IntegrateIT Australia, we provide quality IT services and solutions for all sizes of business. We plan, design and implement all your IT and network needs, working with you to create the best solution given your individual needs. Based in Queensland and Northern New South Wales, we cover Australia nationally supporting clients anywhere. With fast response times, we get you back working as quickly as possible.

IntegrateIT was founded with the intention to better provide IT services and solutions to our clients. The two founders of IntegrateIT have more than 30 years’ experience of combined technical experience from working in a broad array of companies and industries.

We service all industries including:

  • Accountants

  • Solicitors

  • Building / Construction

  • Quantity Surveyors

  • Engineering / Architects

  • Accounting
  • Solicitors
  • Building / Construction
  • Quantity Surveying
  • Architects & Engineering

We provide our services through industry focused staff that are committed to providing you a high level of customer satisfaction and service level but at the same time delivering a personalised service. Our intent is to develop long term business relationships with our clients in which they can have both trust and faith in their IT provider. We strive to excel in this while providing a high level of technical support to our clients. We also have a great ‘word of mouth’ reputation.

Communication is a key issue in IT, where many providers do not communicate well with their clients. At IntegrateIT, we pride ourselves with being able to communicate with our clients and talking to them on a regular basis to ensure everything is working as intended.

Our team of professionals are ready to take your call. Our commitment to you is to keep improving your business and taking your business to the next level.
